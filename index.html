About Me

My name is Luke Tucker - I am 23 years old, and I’m based just outside of Portsmouth. For the past 9 months, I have been self-teaching front end front development, starting with the fundamentals and very recently stepping into React. In this time, I’ve managed to get used to the key elements of front end development and look forward to stepping into the professional workplace.

With this training I’m helping to improve not just my own command of the subject, but how I can interact with others who know the subject. Thanks to this, I’m making significant progress in the professional side of development, becoming more accustomed to the challenges and the demands that are likely to exist as time goes on.

My goal is to become a full stack developer and, one day, transition into the VR development world. As someone filled with ambition to push into new environments that challenge me and force me to improve, I feel confident taking new steps. Each step forward for me is likely to help me to produce my best work for my colleagues and clients alike.

Here, you will find information on the skills I have acquired as well as a selection of projects I have completed. This should help you get a better understanding of what I bring to the table, as well as how I would be an excellent fit for your organization.
