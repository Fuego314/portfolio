We Are JmB-PC

Formed in 2014, JmB-PC came to be as we noticed a real shortage in Purbrook for those with computer expertise. Having seen one too many family friend and colleague run to the ground with PC frustration, we decided to step in and offer a cost-effective, comfortable and friendly alternative to PC repair.

This is a service built to make your life easier – whether you’re a business professional who needs to finish a document or a gamer with a bust graphics card, we’re here to help. Contact us today if you need any assistance with PC repair in Purbrook.
